Content Policy

Effective date: May 19, 2026 · App: ShotMemory · Bundle ID: com.luoyi.ShotMemory

This Content Policy explains expectations for screenshots, photos, notes, OCR text, reminders, exported cards, and other material you create, save, or share with ShotMemory.

No public feed. ShotMemory is a private utility. The App does not provide a public social feed or developer-operated publishing platform. If you export or share a card, you are responsible for that sharing.

1. Your Responsibility

You are responsible for the content you import, capture, save, organize, edit, export, or share. You should only save content when you have the rights and permissions needed to do so.

2. Prohibited Content and Uses

  • Illegal content or content used to facilitate illegal activity.
  • Content that infringes copyrights, trademarks, privacy rights, publicity rights, trade secrets, or other rights.
  • Non-consensual intimate, sexual, private, or exploitative material.
  • Harassment, threats, hate, abuse, doxxing, stalking, or content intended to harm others.
  • Content involving minors in sexual, exploitative, abusive, or unsafe contexts.
  • Instructions or organization of serious physical harm, self-harm, weapon misuse, fraud, or evasion of law enforcement.
  • Malware, credential theft, phishing material, or content intended to compromise systems or accounts.

3. Sensitive Information

Screenshots often contain private information such as messages, receipts, addresses, health details, financial data, credentials, or personal identifiers. Review content carefully before saving, exporting, or sharing cards. Do not store or distribute sensitive information unless you have permission and a lawful reason to do so.

4. OCR and Categories

OCR and categorization are convenience features and may be inaccurate. You should not rely on them as legal, financial, medical, security, or professional advice. Review important cards manually.

5. Reminders and Notifications

If you set a reminder, the local notification may display the card title or note on your device. Avoid putting sensitive information into titles or notes if other people may see your notifications.

6. Sharing and Exports

When you share a designed card through the iOS share sheet or another destination, the destination app or service may process that content under its own policies. You are responsible for choosing appropriate recipients and reviewing exported content before sending it.

7. Enforcement

Because the App is local-first and does not host a public feed, we generally do not review your private on-device content. We may update the App, policies, or App Store listing if we become aware of misuse, legal requirements, or safety risks.

8. Related Policies

This Content Policy is part of the App's legal terms. Please also review the Privacy Policy and Terms of Use.
