Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 26, 2026
App: ContactAudit
Bundle identifier: com.luoyi.contactaudit

This Privacy Policy describes how ContactAudit Team ("we," "us," or "our") handles information when you use ContactAudit (the "App"). ContactAudit is designed to review contacts on your device, identify duplicate or incomplete entries, and help you apply cleanup or restore actions only after your confirmation.

1. Summary

ContactAudit processes your address book on your device. We do not operate a private server for uploading or storing your contacts, and the App does not require an account. Backup records may be stored in your iCloud Documents container when iCloud is available, or locally on your device as a fallback. Purchases are handled by Apple's in-app purchase system.

2. Information the App processes

2.1 Contacts data

After you grant Contacts permission, the App may read contact names, phone numbers, email addresses, and related contact fields needed to detect duplicates, incomplete entries, and restore candidates. The App uses Apple's Contacts framework for this access.

The App may also update, merge, delete, add, or restore contacts when you explicitly approve those actions in the App. Contact cleanup and restore operations are performed through Apple's Contacts framework on your device.

2.2 Backups and restore records

Before cleanup or restore changes, the App can save a restorable vCard snapshot of contacts, together with metadata such as backup title, creation date, contact count, and whether the backup was created from limited Contacts access. Backup files are stored as vCard files and small sidecar metadata files.

When iCloud Documents is available for the App, backup records are saved in the App's iCloud container under a ContactBackups directory. If iCloud is unavailable, the App uses local app storage on your device.

2.3 Operation history and preferences

The App stores recent operation history, such as backups, merges, deletions, restores, purchase unlock events, and test-data actions. This history is stored locally and may be mirrored through Apple's iCloud key-value store when available. The App also stores preferences such as your selected language.

2.4 Purchases

The App offers a one-time ContactAudit Pro purchase through StoreKit. Apple processes payment, entitlement, refund, and transaction information. The App checks the purchase entitlement to unlock Pro features. We do not receive your full payment card details from Apple.

3. What we do not collect

  • We do not require a ContactAudit account.
  • We do not upload your address book to a ContactAudit-owned backend server.
  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not use third-party advertising SDKs in the App as implemented.
  • We do not collect precise location for the App's contact cleanup features.

4. Apple services and device backups

Contacts access, iCloud Documents, iCloud key-value storage, App Store purchases, App Store analytics, crash diagnostics, and device backups are provided by Apple and governed by Apple's terms, privacy policy, your Apple ID settings, and your device settings. For example, iCloud backup behavior depends on your iOS and iCloud configuration.

5. Retention and deletion

Contact data remains in your address book unless you approve a change or delete it through iOS or the App. Backup files and operation history remain until you delete them, remove the App, or manage the relevant iCloud/local storage according to iOS behavior. Removing the App may delete local App data, subject to iOS and iCloud settings.

6. Security

ContactAudit relies on Apple's platform security, Contacts permission controls, local app sandboxing, and iCloud protections where applicable. No method of storage is perfectly secure. You are responsible for protecting your device and Apple ID, including passcode, Face ID or Touch ID, and iCloud account security.

7. Your choices

  • You can grant, limit, or revoke Contacts access in iOS Settings.
  • You can choose whether to apply suggested merge, delete, or restore actions.
  • You can export or remove backup records using App and iOS file-management features where available.
  • You can manage iCloud availability and device backup behavior in iOS Settings.
  • You can restore eligible purchases through the App Store purchase system.

8. Children

The App is not directed to children under 13 or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through a ContactAudit account system because the App does not provide one.

9. Regional privacy rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or limit certain processing of personal information. Because ContactAudit primarily processes data on your device and through Apple services, many choices can be exercised directly in the App or iOS Settings. For requests directed to us, contact us using the information below.
