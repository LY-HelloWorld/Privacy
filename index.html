AI Photo Enhancer Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 1, 2026

Welcome to AI Photo Enhancer ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

1. No Data Collection (Privacy First)

AI Photo Enhancer is designed with a privacy-first approach. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

  • Photos: When you enhance a photo, processing is handled entirely on your device using Apple's Core ML technology. Your photos never leave your device and are never uploaded to any server or shared with third-party services.
  • Personal Data: We do not require account registration. We do not collect your name, email address, or phone number.
  • Usage Data: We do not track your specific usage patterns or location.

2. Photo Processing

All image enhancement is performed using advanced AI models on-device. Your photos remain your own and are never used to train our models or shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. In-App Purchases

All financial transactions are processed securely by Apple via the App Store. We do not have access to your credit card information or billing address. We only receive a confirmation from Apple that a purchase was successful to unlock paid features.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use basic diagnostic tools, such as Apple's built-in crash reporting, to improve app stability. These tools provide anonymous technical data that cannot be used to identify you.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a new effective date.

6. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please use the support link on the App Store product page.
