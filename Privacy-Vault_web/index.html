Hide Photos: Private Vault

Hide Photos: Private Vault helps you hide private photos, videos, PDFs, documents, and ZIP files in a local vault on your iPhone. Import media and files, protect access with Face ID or PIN, and keep sensitive content organized without making it visible in your regular gallery. Key Features: - Hide photos, videos, PDFs, documents, ZIP files, and more - Private camera for direct secure capture - Face ID and PIN lock - Decoy PIN with a separate decoy vault - Calculator, Notes, and Calendar disguise modes - Intruder selfie alerts for failed unlock attempts - Recently Deleted recovery for photos, videos, and files - Local-only storage with backup and restore Your private content stays on your device and under your control.
