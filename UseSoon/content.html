This User Content & Acceptable Use Policy ("Content Policy") explains rules for content you enter, scan, store, or choose to display in UseSoon ("the App"). It supplements our Terms of Use and Privacy Policy.

Important: UseSoon is intended for ordinary household food tracking. Avoid storing highly sensitive information, confidential documents, passwords, government identifiers, or private health information in food names, photos, or custom location fields.

1. Your content

"User content" includes food names, custom storage locations, selected or captured images, expiration dates, quantities, estimated values, reminder settings, and any other information you enter or confirm in the App. You are responsible for your content and for ensuring you have the rights to use it.

2. Where content may appear

Depending on your settings and iOS permissions, your content may appear in:

  • The App's food lists, details, statistics, and history views.
  • Local notifications reminding you about food expiration dates.
  • Apple system permission prompts and App Store purchase flows where relevant.

These displays occur on your device through iOS. You should consider device visibility, notification previews, and shared-device situations before saving content that others should not see.

3. Prohibited content and conduct

You agree not to use the App to create, store, scan, or manage content that:

  • Violates applicable law or promotes illegal activity.
  • Infringes intellectual property, privacy, publicity, or other rights of any person.
  • Contains harassment, hate, threats, or unlawful discriminatory content.
  • Contains sexually explicit content involving minors or any other illegal material.
  • Contains malware instructions, unauthorized credentials, or instructions intended to compromise systems or accounts.
  • Attempts to bypass purchase limits, subscription controls, App Store rules, or Apple platform security.

4. No public sharing feature

The App does not provide a public feed, social profile, or user-to-user content sharing service as a core feature. Content you enter is intended for your personal on-device use. We do not routinely monitor private local food records.

5. Food safety responsibility

UseSoon can help organize dates, but it cannot determine whether food is safe to consume. OCR, reminders, and date calculations may be wrong or incomplete. Always rely on product labels, storage instructions, recalls, appearance, smell, and qualified guidance when making food safety decisions.
