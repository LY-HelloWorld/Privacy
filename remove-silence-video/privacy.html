Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 28, 2026 · App: Remove Silence Video · Bundle ID: com.ly.RemoveSilenceVideo

This Privacy Policy explains how Remove Silence Video Team ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use Remove Silence Video (the "App"). The App helps you import a video, detect silent pauses, create a shorter edited result, and save or share the result.

Local processing. The current App does not require an account, does not use a developer-operated backend for video processing, and does not upload your video or audio files to our servers.

1. Information Processed On Your Device

  • Videos you choose: The App processes only videos that you select through the iOS Photos picker or compatible system import flow.
  • Audio analysis: The App uses on-device audio and video frameworks to identify silent sections and build a shorter output video.
  • Temporary files: Imported and exported video files may be copied to the App's temporary storage while processing is running. Temporary files may be removed by the App or by iOS storage management.
  • Export entitlement state: The App stores local information in UserDefaults to track whether the one free export has been used.
  • Purchase entitlement state: The App checks StoreKit transaction entitlements to determine whether the lifetime unlock is active.

2. Photos Access

The App requests access to import a video you choose and to save an exported result to your photo library when you ask it to do so. The App does not scan your entire photo library or automatically import videos without your action.

3. Purchases

The App offers a non-consumable lifetime unlock for unlimited exports. Purchases, payment processing, receipts, refunds, and purchase restoration are handled by Apple through StoreKit and your Apple ID. We do not receive or store your full payment card details.

4. Information We Do Not Collect

The current implementation does not collect account registration data, contact lists, precise location, advertising identifiers, payment card numbers, or analytics events on our servers. The App does not include third-party advertising SDKs, third-party analytics SDKs, or cross-app tracking.

5. Apple and System Services

When you use iOS Photos, StoreKit, Share Sheet, App Store purchase restoration, or other Apple system services, Apple may process information under Apple's own terms and privacy policies.

6. Retention and Deletion

Temporary imported and exported video files are used for processing and export workflows. The free export counter remains on your device until app data is deleted or reset by iOS. Because the App is local-first, we generally cannot access, restore, or delete data that exists only on your device.

7. Security

The App uses Apple's app sandbox and system permission prompts to limit access to your media. No app or storage method can be guaranteed to be completely secure. You are responsible for controlling access to your device and reviewing exported videos before saving or sharing them.

8. Children

The App is not directed to children under 13 or the minimum age required in your region. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through developer-operated servers for this App.

9. Regional Privacy Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have privacy rights such as access, correction, deletion, portability, objection, restriction, or withdrawal of consent. For information stored only on your device, use the App and iOS controls to manage or delete it. You may contact us for privacy questions using the email below.

10. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy when the App changes or legal requirements evolve. The updated version will be posted at this page with a new effective date.
