Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 29, 2026

Operator: Beauty Expiry Team

App: Beauty Expiry Tracker

Bundle identifier: com.ly.find.beautyexpiry.tool

Overview

Beauty Expiry Tracker helps you keep a private shelf of beauty, skincare, makeup, fragrance, haircare, body, and other personal care products. The app is designed to store your shelf data locally on your device and does not require account registration.

Data We Process

The app may process the following information on your device when you choose to enter or add it:

  • Product records, including product name, brand, category, status, purchase date, opened date, expiry date, period-after-opening months, notes, creation date, and update date.
  • Product photos you select from your photo library.
  • Calculated expiry status, such as whether a product is expired or expiring soon.
  • CSV and JSON export content that you explicitly generate or copy.
  • Local Premium entitlement state read through Apple StoreKit.
  • Language preference and other app preferences stored locally by iOS.

Photos

The app uses photo library access only when you choose a product photo. Selected images are compressed and saved as part of your local shelf record. Beauty Expiry Team does not receive your photo library or product photos.

Local Storage

Your product shelf is saved locally on your device using iOS storage. Your data remains on your device unless you choose to export, copy, share, back up, restore, or otherwise transmit it through Apple system features or other apps.

Exports, Clipboard, and Sharing

The app may let you export or copy shelf data in CSV or JSON format. When you copy, paste, share, save, email, message, or upload exported content outside the app, that destination is controlled by you and may be subject to the privacy practices of the receiving app, service, or person.

Purchases and StoreKit

Beauty Expiry Tracker may offer a one-time Premium unlock through Apple StoreKit using product identifier premium_lifetime_v1. Payment processing, purchase history, refunds, and payment account data are handled by Apple. Beauty Expiry Team does not collect or store your payment card details. The app reads StoreKit transaction entitlement status only to unlock paid features.

No Account, Server, or Analytics in the Current App

The current app implementation does not create a Beauty Expiry account, does not operate a custom backend service for shelf data, and does not include third-party analytics SDKs. If future versions add sync, account features, cloud services, advertising, or analytics, this policy should be updated before release of those features.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your shelf records, notes, photos, exports, or product history with third parties by default. Data leaves your device only when you choose to share, export, copy, back up, or transmit it, or when Apple processes purchase-related information through StoreKit and App Store services.

Data Retention and Deletion

Your local shelf data remains on your device until you edit or delete product records, import replacement data, clear app data, or uninstall the app. Deleting the app generally removes local app data from the device, subject to iOS behavior and any device or iCloud backups you manage.

Product Safety and Health Information

Beauty Expiry Tracker is a personal organization tool. Expiry dates and reminders are calculated from the information you enter and do not constitute medical, dermatological, cosmetic safety, or product quality advice. You remain responsible for checking texture, scent, color, packaging, storage conditions, labels, and professional guidance before using any product.

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal data in a way that requires action, contact us.

International Use

If you are in a jurisdiction with specific privacy rights, you may contact us for assistance. Because the app is designed for local on-device storage, many privacy actions can be performed directly by editing, exporting, or deleting data on your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material updates will be reflected by changing the effective date.
