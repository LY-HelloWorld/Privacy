What the app is built for

WorkProofCam is positioned for contractors, installers, cleaners, and field teams that need organized proof photos and shareable reports without a backend workflow.

Keep every photo attached to the right job

Job titles, client names, site names, notes, and proof photo labels stay organized locally on the device.

Capture before and after evidence

Track before, after, and general proof photos so each report keeps the visual sequence that matters on-site.

Add time, address, and location context

When permission is granted, reports can include timestamp, GPS, and reverse-geocoded address details for stronger proof.

Export a clean PDF report

Generate PDF job reports from local job, photo, note, timestamp, and address data, then share them through iOS.

Common questions

Is WorkProofCam only for contractors?

No. It is useful anywhere you need proof photos with notes and a report, including cleaning, inspections, repairs, and service visits.

Does the app upload my photos?

The current implementation is designed for local-first storage. Data leaves the device only when you choose to export or share a report.

What is the main output?

The main output is a PDF photo report built from the local job record, photos, notes, timestamps, and optional location details.
