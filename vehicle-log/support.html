Common Topics

VehicleLog stores vehicle profiles, maintenance records, reminders, and receipt attachments locally on your device. If you delete the app, local data may be removed by iOS unless you have a backup you control.

VehicleLog Pro purchases are processed by Apple. Use Restore Purchases in the app if you reinstall VehicleLog or move to another device with the same Apple Account.
