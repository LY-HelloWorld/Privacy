Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 11, 2026
App: Deposit Defender
Bundle identifier: com.ly.DepositDefender

This Privacy Policy explains how Deposit Defender ("the App," "we," "us," or "our") handles information when you use the iOS application. Deposit Defender is operated by the Deposit Defender Team.

1. Summary

Deposit Defender helps renters document move-in and move-out property condition with room-by-room notes, photos, issue tags, and PDF reports. The App is designed to keep rental projects, inspection records, photos, and reports local to your device. The current App does not require an account, does not provide cloud sync, does not include advertising SDKs, and does not intentionally send your inspection records to our servers.

2. Information the App processes

2.1 Rental projects and inspection data

The App stores records locally using Apple on-device storage. This may include:

  • Property address, property type, move-in date, and optional move-out date.
  • Inspection type, status, room names, checklist items, completion progress, and timestamps.
  • Issue notes, evidence tags such as pre-existing damage or cleaning needed, severity levels, and whether an issue is marked as pre-existing.
  • Generated PDF report records and export timestamps.

2.2 Photos

When you use the camera or import images from your photo library, the App stores JPEG image files in the App's private Application Support directory on your device. These photos are used to show inspection evidence and generate PDF reports.

2.3 Camera and photo library permissions

The App requests camera access so you can take room-by-room evidence photos. The App requests photo library access so you can import existing property photos. These permissions are controlled by iOS Settings.

2.4 PDF reports and sharing

The App generates PDF reports from local project, inspection, note, tag, severity, timestamp, and photo data. When you export or share a report, you choose the destination, such as Mail, Files, AirDrop, Messages, or another app. Deposit Defender does not upload reports to its own server.

2.5 In-app purchases

The App may offer a one-time Pro unlock through Apple's StoreKit framework, including the product identifier depositdefender.pro.unlock. Purchases, billing, refunds, transaction verification, and purchase management are handled by Apple. We receive only the purchase status needed to unlock App features on your device.

3. Local storage, retention, and deletion

Rental projects, inspection records, photos, and reports remain on your device until you delete them where the App provides deletion controls, remove the App, or reset your device, subject to iOS backup and restore behavior. Apple device backups, iCloud Backup, and device-level retention are controlled by Apple and your device settings.

4. No accounts, analytics, advertising, or cloud sync in v1

The App as currently implemented does not create user accounts, does not operate cloud storage for inspection records, does not include third-party analytics SDKs, and does not include advertising SDKs. Apple may provide aggregated App Store analytics, crash information, and purchase reporting to developers through Apple systems; those are governed by Apple's policies and your device settings.

5. Third-party and Apple services

The App relies on Apple platform services including SwiftData, PhotosUI, UIKit camera and PDF rendering APIs, StoreKit, and standard iOS sharing surfaces. Your use of Apple services is also subject to Apple's terms and privacy practices.

6. Security

We rely on Apple's platform security controls for on-device data. No storage method is completely secure. You are responsible for securing your device with appropriate safeguards such as a passcode, Face ID, Touch ID, trusted backups, and careful sharing choices.

7. Your choices

  • You can deny or revoke camera and photo library permissions in iOS Settings.
  • You choose what property details, notes, and photos to add.
  • You choose when and where to export or share PDF reports.
  • You can remove App-local data by deleting records where controls are available or uninstalling the App, subject to iOS backup behavior.
  • You can manage purchases through your Apple ID account settings.

8. Children's privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through our own servers. If you believe a child has provided information in a way that concerns you, contact us.

9. Regional privacy rights

Depending on where you live, you may have privacy rights such as access, deletion, correction, or objection to certain processing. Because the App is designed around local device storage, many requests can be addressed directly on your device. For requests directed to us, contact us using the information below.

10. Changes to this policy
