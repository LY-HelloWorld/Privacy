Frequently Asked Questions

How does AI restoration work?

ClearPix uses deep learning models (CoreML) that have been trained on millions of high-quality images to predict and reconstruct missing details in your photos.

Are my photos uploaded to a server?

No. All processing happens locally on your device using on-device machine learning. Your privacy is our priority—your photos never leave your device.

How can I subscribe to Pro features?

You can access Pro features through the store icon in the app. We offer a one-time lifetime purchase with no recurring fees.

Why did the restoration fail for certain photos?

Heavily blurred or extremely low-quality photos may be harder for the AI to reconstruct. We recommend using photos with at least some visible facial features for best results.
