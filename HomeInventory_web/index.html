Features

Everything needed to find packed items later.

01

Organize boxes by room

Create moving boxes or storage boxes, assign rooms, mark packed status, and keep a clean list of what still needs attention.

02

Add item photos and notes

Record item names, quantities, categories, fragile flags, valuable flags, notes, and photos so each box has useful context.

03

Print QR labels

Generate QR labels for boxes and export printable label PDFs for Letter or A4 paper. Scan a label to open the matching box record.

04

Search and filter fast

Find boxes and items by name, room, category, or notes. Filter by room and packed status during a move or unpacking day.

05

Export PDF or CSV

Share or save your inventory as CSV or PDF, including useful summaries for moving, storage, insurance, or household records.

06

Track packing progress

See total boxes, packed boxes, unpacked boxes, item counts, room summaries, fragile items, and valuable items from the dashboard.

FAQ

Common questions about HomeInventory.

Is HomeInventory only for moving?

No. It is focused on moving box inventory, but it also works for storage boxes, garage bins, seasonal items, and home organization.

Can I print QR labels for boxes?

Yes. HomeInventory can generate box QR labels and export printable label PDFs for Letter or A4 paper.

Where is my inventory data stored?

Your inventory records and item photos are stored locally on your device. Data leaves the device only when you choose to export or share files.

Can I export my box inventory?

Yes. You can export inventory reports as CSV or PDF, and you can also export printable QR label PDFs.
