Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 9, 2026

Privacy Vault ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use the Privacy Vault mobile application (the "App"), a local-only privacy tool for storing photos, videos and documents.

This Privacy Policy is publicly available at: https://ly-helloworld.github.io/Privacy/Privacy_vault/privacy_policy

1. Local-Only Storage Policy

CRITICAL NOTICE

Privacy Vault is a strictly "Local-Only" utility. Your photos, videos, documents and all other personal media stored in the App are saved exclusively on your mobile device's local storage.

  • We do NOT use any form of cloud storage for your personal data.
  • We do NOT transmit, upload or share your private media library to any external servers, third parties or our own systems.
  • We have no access whatsoever to the data you store within the App.

Due to the local-only storage mechanism, deleting or uninstalling the App from your device will result in the permanent loss of all data stored in it. We strongly recommend using the App's "Export" feature to back up your data before any uninstallation or device reset.

2. Information We Collect

We adhere to a minimal data collection principle and do not require any account registration, login or personal information submission to use the App. All collected data is anonymous, non-identifiable and only used to improve App stability and performance:

  • Biometric Data: If you enable Face ID (iOS) or Touch ID/Fingerprint ID (Android) for App authentication, the entire biometric verification process is handled exclusively by your device's native system. We do not receive, process or store any of your actual biometric data (e.g., facial features, fingerprint information).
  • Camera Access: Camera permission is only required for the App's "Intruder Capture" feature. All photos captured by this feature are stored locally within the App and are never transmitted, shared or uploaded to any external party.
  • Photo Library Access: Photo library permission is only used to import your photos/videos into the App and export them back to your device's native gallery. We do not access, view or store any unselected media in your photo library.
  • Anonymous Usage Data: We may collect non-identifiable technical information (e.g., device model, operating system version, App crash logs) in an anonymous form. This data is used solely to fix bugs, optimize App performance and improve user experience, and cannot be used to identify individual users.

3. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with limited third-party services only to provide core payment and system-level functionality, and these services do not have access to your personal media data stored in the App. All third-party services have their own independent privacy policies:

  • App Store (iOS) / Google Play (Android): These platforms are only used to process the one-time purchase payment for the App (no recurring subscriptions or auto-renewals). The payment process and your payment information are managed exclusively by Apple/Google, and we do not receive or store any of your payment details (e.g., credit card information, account balance).
    • Apple App Store Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/en-ww/
    • Google Play Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Local Authentication Frameworks (iOS/Android): Native system-level frameworks used for PIN code and biometric authentication. These frameworks only run on your local device and do not transmit any authentication data externally.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your local data from unauthorized access on your device:

  • File-level encryption is applied to all data stored in the App for all users (no Pro/paid tier restriction).
  • All authentication methods (PIN code, biometrics) are tied to your local device only.

You are solely responsible for maintaining the secrecy of your App PIN code and protecting your mobile device from unauthorized physical access. We cannot retrieve or reset your PIN code, and any loss of PIN code may result in inability to access your data stored in the App.

5. Your Rights

Since all your data is stored exclusively on your local device, you have full and complete control over all data in the App. Your rights include:

  • Access, view, edit, export or delete your data stored in the App at any time through the App's native interface.
  • Revoke any App permissions (e.g., camera, photo library) at any time in your device's system settings, without affecting the normal use of other App functions (where applicable).
  • Uninstall the App at any time, which will permanently remove all local data stored in the App (please back up data first).

We do not store any of your data on external servers, so there is no need for data deletion requests to our team—all data management is completed on your local device.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect, process or store any personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has used the App to store sensitive data, please contact us using the information in Section 7.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may occasionally update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any material changes will be posted within the App and on the public Privacy Policy webpage (the link above) with an updated "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the App after the changes take effect constitutes your acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.
