Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 9, 2026

Privacy Vault ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use the Privacy Vault mobile application.

1. Local-Only Storage Policy

CRITICAL NOTICE: Privacy Vault is a "Local-Only" utility. Your photos, videos, and documents are stored exclusively on your device's local storage.

  • We do NOT use cloud storage.
  • We do NOT transmit your private library to any external servers.
  • We do NOT have access to the data you store within the vault.

Because your data is stored locally, deleting the app will result in the permanent loss of all data stored within it. We recommend using the "Export" feature before uninstalling the application.

2. Information We Collect

We believe in minimal data collection. We do not require account registration.

  • Biometric Data: If you enable Face ID or Touch ID, the authentication is handled entirely by the iOS/Android system. We do not receive or store your actual biometric data.
  • Camera Access: The camera is used for the "Intruder Capture" feature. Captured photos are stored locally within the app and are never shared.
  • Photo Library Access: We require permission to import photos into the vault and export them back to your system gallery.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous, non-identifiable technical information (e.g., device model, OS version) to improve app stability and performance.

3. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with certain third-party services to provide core functionality:

  • App Store / Google Play: For handling "Pro" subscriptions and payments.
  • Local Authentication Support: System-level frameworks for PIN and Biometric security.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including file-level encryption for "Pro" users, to protect your data from unauthorized access on your device. However, you are responsible for maintaining the secrecy of your PIN code.

5. Your Rights

Since all data is on your device, you have full control. You can access, modify, or delete your data at any time through the app interface. To withdraw all permissions, you can adjust your device settings or uninstall the app.

6. Children's Privacy

Our application does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has used the app to store sensitive data, please contact us.
