Your guide to using Privacy Vault – local encrypted photo vault for iOS

1. Quick Start Guide

How to Set Up Privacy Vault

  1. Download and open the Privacy Vault app on your iPhone/iPad.
  2. On first launch, tap GET STARTED and set a 6-digit main PIN (enter twice to confirm).
  3. Optionally set a Decoy PIN (or tap Skip). Entering the decoy PIN opens a separate, empty vault – useful for privacy (Pro feature).
  4. Enable Face ID / Touch ID in Settings > Security > Biometrics for quick unlock.
  5. From the gallery, tap the + button to import photos/videos from your device. All files are encrypted locally with AES-256.

How to Use Core Features

  • Import media: In the vault gallery, tap + > choose photos/videos from your library > confirm. Files are copied into the vault and encrypted.
  • Export media: Open a photo/video > use the share button to save to your device gallery; or use Select mode to multi-select and export in batch (Pro).
  • Albums: Tap the album icon in the top left to switch or create albums. Creating multiple albums requires Pro.
  • Intruder Capture (Pro): In Settings > VIP Features, enable Intruder Capture. After 1 wrong PIN attempt, the app takes a photo with the front camera. View entries in Intrusion Logs.
  • Decoy Vault (Pro): Set a Decoy PIN in Settings. When someone enters the decoy PIN at unlock, they see an empty vault instead of your real one.
  • Backup & Restore: Settings > Data Management > Export Backup (ZIP) to create an encrypted backup; use Restore from Backup to restore from a ZIP file (password required).

2. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: I forgot my main PIN. How can I recover it?

A: We cannot recover your PIN – all data is stored and encrypted locally on your device. If you forget your main PIN:

  • You will not be able to access your vault. Uninstalling and reinstalling the app will permanently delete all vault data.
  • We strongly recommend writing down your PIN and keeping it in a secure place.

Q: My data disappeared after updating the app or my device. What should I do?

A: Privacy Vault keeps all data only on your device. Data loss can happen if:

  • You uninstalled the app without exporting or backing up first.
  • Your device was reset or restored without a backup that included the app data.

We recommend using Export Backup (ZIP) in Settings before any major update or restore, and keeping that backup in a safe place.

Q: Does Privacy Vault sync my data to the cloud?

A: No. Privacy Vault is 100% local – we do not sync, backup, or upload your data to any server. Everything stays on your iPhone or iPad.

Q: How do I activate the Pro version?

A: When a Pro feature is locked (e.g. Decoy Vault, Intruder Capture), tap it to open the paywall. Choose Buy Lifetime Pro ($4.99) and complete the purchase in the App Store. You can also tap Restore Purchases if you already bought Pro on another device or after reinstall. Pro includes: unlimited storage, unlimited albums, Decoy Vault, AES-256 encryption, and batch management.

Q: Can I get a refund for the Pro version?

A: Refunds are handled by the Apple App Store. Please submit a refund request through your Apple ID account (typically within the period allowed by Apple’s policy).

3. Troubleshooting

App Crashes When Opening

  • Close other apps and restart Privacy Vault.
  • Update iOS to the latest version.
  • If the problem continues, reinstall the app (export or back up your data first if you can still access the vault).

Cannot Import Photos or Videos

  • Grant full access to your photo library: Settings > Privacy & Security > Photos > Privacy Vault > All Photos (or Limited with the albums you need).
  • Ensure files are in supported formats (e.g. JPG, PNG, HEIC, MP4, MOV) and not corrupted.

Intruder Capture Not Working

  • Confirm Privacy Vault has camera permission: Settings > Privacy & Security > Camera > Privacy Vault > On.
  • In the app, go to Settings > VIP Features > Intruder Capture and turn it on (Pro required).
  • The photo is taken after 1 wrong PIN attempt on the unlock screen.
