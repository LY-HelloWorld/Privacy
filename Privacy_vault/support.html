1. Quick Start Guide

How to Set Up Pic Safe

  1. Download and open the Pic Safe app.
  2. Set a 4-6 digit PIN code (this is your main access password).
  3. Optional: Enable Face ID/Touch ID for quick access (Settings > Biometric Authentication).
  4. Tap the "+" button to import photos/videos from your device's gallery.
  5. All imported files are automatically encrypted and stored locally on your device.

How to Use Core Features

  • Import Media: Tap "+" > Select "Import Photos/Videos" > Choose files > Confirm.
  • Export Media: Long-press a file > Select "Export" > Choose destination (gallery).
  • Intruder Photo Capture: Enable in Settings > The app will take a photo when someone enters the wrong PIN 3 times.
  • Fake Passcode (Pro): Set a secondary PIN in Settings > Entering this PIN shows an empty vault.

2. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: I forgot my PIN code. How can I recover it?

A: We cannot recover your PIN code (all data is stored locally). If you forget your PIN:

  • Uninstall and reinstall the app (note: all stored data will be permanently deleted).
  • We strongly recommend writing down your PIN in a secure place.

Q: My data disappeared after updating the app/device. What should I do?

A: Pic Safe stores all data locally on your device. Data loss may occur if:

  • You uninstalled the app accidentally.
  • Your device was reset or updated (without backup).
  • We recommend exporting all important files to your gallery before updating the app/OS.

Q: Does Pic Safe sync my data to the cloud?

A: No. Pic Safe is a 100% local storage app – we do not sync, backup, or upload your data to any cloud servers or our databases. Your data stays only on your device.

Q: How to activate the Pro version?

A: Tap "Upgrade to Pro" in the app > Select "Lifetime Pro" > Complete payment via App Store/Google Play > Restart the app to unlock all Pro features (AES-256 encryption, intruder capture, fake passcode).

Q: Can I get a refund for the Pro version?

A: Refunds are handled by Apple App Store or Google Play Store. Please submit a refund request through your app store account (usually within 14 days of purchase).

3. Troubleshooting

App Crashes When Opening

  • Close all background apps and restart Pic Safe.
  • Update your device's operating system to the latest version.
  • Reinstall the app (backup your data first if possible).

Cannot Import Photos/Videos

  • Check if Pic Safe has permission to access your photo library (Device Settings > Pic Safe > Photos > Allow Full Access).
  • Make sure the files are not corrupted or in an unsupported format (supported: JPG, PNG, MP4, MOV).

Intruder Capture Not Working

  • Ensure Pic Safe has camera permission (Device Settings > Pic Safe > Camera > Allow).
  • Verify the feature is enabled in the app's Settings > Intruder Capture.
  • The feature triggers only after 3 consecutive wrong PIN attempts.
