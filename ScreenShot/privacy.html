Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 23, 2025 · App: App Store Screenshot Studio

This Privacy Policy describes how [REPLACE: Your legal name or entity] (“we”, “us”) handles information in connection with the mobile application App Store Screenshot Studio (the “App”). It is intended to meet common expectations for App Store distribution, including transparency aligned with Apple’s App Review Guidelines and privacy labeling practices.

1. Scope

This policy applies to the App on iOS/iPadOS. It does not apply to third-party websites or services that we do not operate.

2. Information we do not collect on our servers

The App is designed to help you compose and export App Store-style screenshots using templates and images you choose. We do not operate a user account system in the App for the core features described above, and we do not require you to upload your photos or exported images to our servers for those features. Processing needed to render and export screenshots occurs on your device, subject to the permissions you grant.

3. Information processed on your device

  • Photos and images: If you grant access, the App may read images you select from your photo library (or from the image picker) to place them into templates. Exported images may be saved to your photo library or shared using the system share sheet when you choose to do so.
  • App-generated files: Temporary or exported files (for example PNG or ZIP) may be stored locally on your device or passed to apps you select via the share sheet.

If the App offers in-app purchases or subscriptions, payment and related transaction information are processed by Apple as described in Apple’s terms and privacy notices. We do not receive your full payment card details from Apple. We may receive limited purchase-related information from Apple that is necessary to validate entitlements (for example, subscription status), as permitted by Apple’s systems.

5. Analytics, advertising, and tracking

Unless we notify you otherwise in the App or in an updated policy, we do not use third-party advertising SDKs in the App and we do not sell your personal information. If we introduce analytics or similar technologies in the future, we will update this policy and, where required, App Store privacy details and consent flows.
