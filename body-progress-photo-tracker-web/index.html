Common questions

Is this only for fitness photos?

No. The same check-in workflow can work for hairline, skin, treatment, and other private visual progress tracking.

Does it upload my progress photos?

The current app is described as local-first. Photos stay on-device unless you explicitly export or share them.

What makes it different from a normal album?

The app is built around consistent framing, repeated check-ins, and side-by-side progress review rather than general photo storage.
