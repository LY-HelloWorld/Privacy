Content Policy

Effective date: May 1, 2026 · App: Rune Spire · Bundle ID: com.ly.runespire.magic

This Content Policy explains rules for content, conduct, and user responsibilities in Rune Spire (the "App") by Rune Spire Team. It supplements the Terms of Use and Privacy Policy.

1. App content

Rune Spire includes fantasy dungeon gameplay, pixel-readable board art, monsters, keys, doors, potions, gems, equipment, shops, trainers, and tower progression text. The App is intended for strategy and puzzle entertainment.

2. User-generated content

The current App does not provide a public feed, chat, account profile, multiplayer posting system, or developer-hosted content upload feature. Gameplay choices and settings are local to the device. If a future version adds user-generated content or online sharing features, additional rules may apply.

3. Local visibility

Information generated by gameplay, such as selected settings, progress, route choices, combat state, or on-screen messages, may be visible to anyone who can access your device or view your screen. The App does not currently publish this information to a public service operated by us.

4. Prohibited misuse

You may not use the App, its name, its artwork, or related materials to:

  • violate applicable law or third-party rights;
  • harass, threaten, defraud, impersonate, or mislead others;
  • promote hate, exploitation, self-harm, malware, or illegal activity;
  • extract, redistribute, or sell App assets except where permitted by law or applicable licenses;
  • misrepresent unofficial modifications, copies, or screenshots as official releases from Rune Spire Team.

5. Screenshots, streaming, and commentary

You may share ordinary gameplay screenshots, short clips, reviews, commentary, and non-commercial fan discussion, provided your use complies with law, platform rules, and these policies. We may ask you to stop using App materials in a way that is misleading, unlawful, abusive, or commercially exploitative.

6. Safety and reporting

Because the App does not host a public community or user uploads in the current version, we do not routinely moderate user content. If you believe App-related content violates rights, misuses our materials, or creates legal or safety concerns, contact us with enough detail to review the issue.

7. Changes

We may update this Content Policy as the App changes, including if new online, purchase, sharing, or community features are added.
