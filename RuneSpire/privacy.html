Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 1, 2026 · App: Rune Spire · Bundle ID: com.ly.runespire.magic

This Privacy Policy explains how Rune Spire Team ("we", "us") handles information in connection with the mobile game Rune Spire (the "App"). This policy is written for the iOS App Store version of the App.

1. Summary

Rune Spire is designed as an offline single-player dungeon puzzle RPG. Based on the current App implementation, the App does not require an account, does not use developer-operated servers for gameplay, does not include advertising SDKs, and does not request camera, photo library, microphone, location, or tracking permission.

2. Information stored on your device

The App may store limited gameplay and settings information locally on your device, including:

  • selected language;
  • master volume and sound effect or background music settings;
  • local gameplay state that is necessary to run the tower, floors, items, monsters, and route choices.

This information is used to provide the game experience and remember your preferences. It is not used to identify you personally.

3. Information we do not collect

For the current version of the App, we do not collect the following through developer-operated systems:

  • name, email address, phone number, or account profile;
  • precise location;
  • contacts, photos, videos, microphone audio, or camera input;
  • advertising identifiers or cross-app tracking data;
  • gameplay analytics sent to our servers.

4. Apple services and purchases

The App currently includes purchase-related interface text and a visual purchase flow preview, but the reviewed code does not implement a real StoreKit purchase transaction. If a future version adds real in-app purchases, billing and transaction processing will be handled by Apple, and we will update this policy and the App Store privacy information where required.

5. No advertising or tracking

The current App does not use third-party advertising SDKs and does not track you across apps or websites owned by other companies. If this changes, we will update this policy and request consent where required by law or Apple policy.

6. Data retention and deletion

Information stored locally remains on your device unless you change settings, delete local game data through available device or app controls, or uninstall the App. Uninstalling the App may remove locally stored App data, subject to iOS behavior and backups.

7. Children

The App is a general-audience game and is not directed to children under 13 or the minimum age required in your region. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through developer-operated servers.

8. Security

We use reasonable measures for systems we control. Because the current App is designed for local play and does not upload gameplay data to our servers, your device security and Apple platform protections are important parts of protecting local data.

9. International users

If you contact us by email, your message may be processed in the country where our email provider or support systems operate. We will use support communications only to respond to your request and maintain appropriate records.

10. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of personal information that we control. Because core App data is stored locally, many controls are available directly on your device. You may contact us for privacy requests.

11. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted at this URL with a new effective date. Material changes may also be reflected in the App Store listing or in the App where appropriate.

12. Contact

For privacy questions, contact Rune Spire Team at luoyi9932@gmail.com. You may also contact us through App Store support channels where available.
